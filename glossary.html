What to know

  • E10: standard petrol containing up to 10% ethanol.
  • B7: standard diesel containing up to 7% biodiesel.
  • MPG: miles per gallon, a common UK fuel economy measure.
  • p/kWh: pence per kilowatt-hour, often used for EV charging.
