Privacy policy

Driverz aims to keep privacy simple. The site should collect only what is useful for providing and improving the service.

Information you provide

If you use the contact form, you may provide your name, email address, enquiry type, subject and message. This information is used to respond to your enquiry.

Location information

If you choose to use current location, your browser may share approximate coordinates so Driverz can show nearby fuel or EV results. You can deny location access and search manually instead.

Location preferences may be stored locally in your browser so Driverz can remember your selected area, radius and fuel type next time.

Calculator inputs

Calculator pages may process values such as mileage, MPG, fuel price, electricity price, battery size or trip distance. Some tools may store entries in your browser only, for example mileage tracker entries, unless a future feature clearly says otherwise.

Server and technical information

Like most websites, hosting providers may process technical information such as IP address, browser type, device information, timestamps, requested pages and error logs for security, performance and reliability.

Cookies and browser storage

Driverz may use cookies and local browser storage to support basic functionality, remember preferences and improve the service.

  • Essential storage: used for settings such as selected location, fuel type, radius, EV connector preference or cookie choice.
  • Analytics cookies: may be used after analytics is added to understand general usage and improve performance.
  • Advertising cookies: may be used in the future if advertising networks are added, subject to consent where required.

You can clear cookies and local storage in your browser settings. If you do this, Driverz may forget your saved preferences.

Data accuracy

Driverz uses available data sources and calculations to make driving information easier to understand. However, real-world information changes quickly.

  • Fuel prices may change before the site updates.
  • EV charger prices, availability and connector details may be missing or operator-dependent.
  • Opening hours may change due to holidays, maintenance or local conditions.
  • Distance and cost estimates depend on assumptions and may not match real driving conditions.
  • Clean air zone, congestion charge, tax and driving rule information should be checked against official sources.

Before making an important journey, payment or legal decision, check the station, operator or official authority directly.

Your choices

You can control many privacy choices from your browser and device settings.

  • Allow or block location permission.
  • Clear saved location, radius, fuel type and calculator entries from browser storage.
  • Accept or reject non-essential cookies if a consent banner is shown.
  • Use the contact page for privacy questions or data correction requests.
