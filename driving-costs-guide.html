Driving Costs Guide

Understand the main costs of running a UK car.

Quick answer: The real cost of driving is fuel plus insurance, tax, MOT, tyres, servicing, parking, tolls and depreciation.

What to know

  • Track pence per mile, not just monthly fuel spend.
  • Tyres and servicing affect both safety and MPG.
  • Use Driverz tools to compare cost changes quickly.
