About Driverz

Fast UK driving tools, without the clutter.

Driverz is built to help UK drivers make quick decisions: find nearby fuel, compare running costs, understand key rules and access useful driver services.

How to use Driverz

1. Search your area

Enter a town, city or postcode, or tap the location button to find nearby fuel stations.

2. Choose fuel type

Switch between petrol, diesel and EV. Tap the mini price pill to cycle quickly.

3. Estimate journey cost

Use the tools to estimate trip cost, CPM, EV charging and petrol comparisons.

4. Keep useful pages ready

Emergency contacts and UK driver services are designed to be simple under pressure.

Design principle

Driverz should feel closer to a utility dashboard than a blog. The goal is simple: search first, useful result second, extra tools only when needed.
