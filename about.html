About Driverz

Built for simple UK driving decisions

Driverz UK is designed to help drivers make quick choices: where to refuel, what to compare nearby, and where to find essential help without clutter.

What Driverz UK focuses on

Fuel prices first

The homepage puts fuel search and comparison close to the top so visitors can act immediately.

Practical tools

Utilities support trip costs, EV charging decisions and common UK driving checks.

Plain-English guidance

Guides and glossary pages explain fuel, EV and road terms simply.

Privacy approach

Location is not requested automatically on page load. Visitors can search manually or choose the location button. Analytics only load after analytics cookies are accepted.

Roadmap

The site is prepared for scheduled fuel CSV updates, EV connector filtering, a 3-mile comparison view, more UK driving guides and future traffic or route-support integrations.
