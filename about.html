About Driverz

Fast UK driving tools, without the clutter.

Driverz is a simple UK driving utility site built around quick answers: nearby fuel prices, driving cost calculators, EV charging estimates, clean air zone guidance, key driving rules and emergency information.

Driverz is designed to feel more like a useful dashboard than a blog. Open it, get the driving information you need, and move on.

What Driverz helps with

Most drivers do not need a long article when they are checking fuel, planning a trip or trying to understand a charge. Driverz keeps the useful part first.

Data and accuracy

Fuel, EV and driving information can change quickly. Driverz is designed to make available data easier to understand, but prices, opening hours, charger details and rules should always be checked before making important decisions.

EV charging data can be incomplete because connector details, live pricing and availability depend on what each charging data source provides.

Driverz is an independent utility site. It is not an official government service, fuel retailer, charging network or emergency service provider.

Privacy approach

Location is used to show nearby driving information. Where possible, preferences such as selected location, radius, fuel type and EV settings are stored in your browser so the site feels faster next time.

Future analytics and cookie settings should be handled with a small, clear consent choice, not a disruptive full-screen popup.
