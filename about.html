About Driverz UK

Built for simple driving decisions.

Driverz UK helps drivers quickly compare fuel, understand options nearby and find useful UK driving information without clutter.

What Driverz UK is for

The aim is to make the first visit immediately useful: fuel search first, comparison second, supporting guides and emergency information nearby.

The site is designed to work with your GitHub/Vercel fuel data workflow and can grow into traffic alerts, route planning and local driving tools later.

Clean homepage

Fuel Finder sits in the hero so visitors see the main value immediately.

Useful comparison

Nearby cards and a compare table help people choose quickly.

Privacy aware

Location is requested only after a user chooses it, and analytics loads after consent.

Expandable

The structure supports future maps, traffic alerts and deeper local guides.
