FAQ

Frequently asked questions.

Quick answers about fuel data, EV filters, location permissions and privacy.

Where does the fuel price data come from?

The site is prepared to use official UK fuel price CSV data through your scheduled GitHub workflow.

Does Driverz UK automatically fetch my location?

No. Location is only requested when the visitor presses “Use my location”. Visitors can type a postcode or town instead.

Why is there a 3-mile compare button?

Three miles is a useful default for local fuel decisions. It keeps the list small and practical while still allowing radius changes.

Can EV drivers filter by connector?

Yes. The interface includes connector filtering for Type 2, CCS, CHAdeMO and Tesla compatible options. Live charging availability would need a suitable charging data provider later.

How are analytics handled?

Google Analytics and Vercel Analytics are only loaded after the visitor accepts analytics cookies.

Is this a replacement for emergency services?

No. Emergency pages are quick guidance only. In immediate danger, call 999.
