Fuel Guide

A simple UK guide to petrol, diesel, E10, E5 and premium fuel.

Quick answer: Use E10 for most petrol cars made since 2011 unless your vehicle requires E5. Diesel drivers should compare B7 prices and avoid misfuelling.

What to know

  • Petrol E10 is the standard grade at most UK forecourts.
  • E5 often costs more and is mainly useful for incompatible or specialist vehicles.
  • Premium fuel may help some engines, but it is not automatically better value for every car.
