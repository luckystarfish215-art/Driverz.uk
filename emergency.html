Emergency contacts

Quick help for UK drivers

Immediate danger

Call 999 if anyone is injured, in danger, or a live motorway lane is blocked.

Emergency services

Call 999 for police, ambulance or fire when there is immediate danger.

Non-emergency police

Call 101 for non-urgent police matters.

Motorway breakdown

Move left if safe, exit the vehicle from the left side, stand behind the barrier, and call for help.

Minor collision

Exchange details safely. Record registration, insurer, location, time and photos if it is safe.
