Emergency contacts

Quick help for UK drivers.

Use this page as a fast reference. In immediate danger, call the relevant official emergency service.

Emergency

999

Police, fire, ambulance or immediate danger.

Non-emergency police

101

Report non-urgent road incidents or crime.

NHS non-emergency

111

Medical advice when it is not a 999 emergency.

Motorway breakdown

Pull over safely, switch on hazards, leave from the safe side, move behind a barrier and call your breakdown provider or emergency services if unsafe.

Roadside safety checklist

  • Stop as far left as safely possible.
  • Switch on hazard lights.
  • Do not stand between vehicles.
  • On a motorway, move behind the barrier if safe.
  • Call for help and give your exact location.
