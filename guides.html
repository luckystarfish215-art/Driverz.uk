How to compare fuel prices properly

Check distance, fuel type and update time together. The cheapest station is not always the best choice if the detour uses more fuel than you save.

EV

Choosing the right EV connector

Type 2 is common for slower AC charging. CCS is common for rapid DC charging. CHAdeMO appears on some older EVs. Always check your car compatibility before travelling.
