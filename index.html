About

Built for simple UK driving decisions

Driverz UK is designed to reduce clutter and help drivers make quick choices: where to refuel, what to compare nearby, and where to find essential help.

The site is prepared for fuel price CSV updates from your GitHub/Vercel workflow and can be expanded later with maps, traffic alerts and account-free saved preferences.

FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Where does the fuel price data come from?

The site is structured to use official UK fuel price CSV data once connected through the scheduled GitHub workflow.

Do I need to share my location?

No. You can type a town or postcode. Location is only requested when you press “Use my location”.

Why compare within 3 miles?

Three miles is a practical default for local driving. You can increase the radius when planning a longer journey.

Does the EV connector filter show live charger availability?

Not yet. The current interface prepares the EV search flow. Live availability can be added later with a suitable charging data provider.

How are cookies and analytics handled?

Analytics scripts only load after a visitor accepts analytics cookies using the consent banner.
